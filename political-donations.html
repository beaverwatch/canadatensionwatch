What this means
Political money surge signals two possible realities — and both are dangerous.
Scenario A — Rising political engagement: Canadians feel institutions are failing and are trying to influence them through legal channels. This sounds healthy but historically precedes major political ruptures.

Scenario B — Elite capture: Wealthy Canadians and corporations are concentrating political funding to protect economic interests. Combined with the CEO wage ratio and housing crisis, this suggests political power is being captured by economic elites — a documented precursor to democratic erosion. The fact that top 5% of donors represent 78% of volume points strongly toward Scenario B.

Methodology
