🔗 How this indicator works
01
Wages don't cover expenses — families turn to payday loans to bridge the gap between paychecks. This is the first visible sign of liquidity stress.
02
Loan amounts and frequency increase — borrowers take larger loans more often, entering a debt spiral. FCAC tracks this in real time.
03
Credit card defaults follow — 8 to 12 weeks after payday loan spikes, credit card delinquency rates rise. Source: Equifax Canada quarterly data.
04
Personal bankruptcies peak — 12 to 16 weeks after signal activation. Documented correlation r=0.78 between payday loan volume and insolvency filings. Source: BDO Canada + FCAC 2023.
