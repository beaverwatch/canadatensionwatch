🔗 How this indicator works
01
Food prices rise faster than wages — families begin cutting food budgets first. Luxury foods go first, then nutritious options.
02
Breakfast Club registrations spike — parents can no longer send children to school with a full lunch. This is the earliest visible signal.
03
Food bank usage rises — critically, first-time users from the middle class signal that the crisis has spread beyond low-income households.
04
Social and political consequences follow — documented 4 to 8 weeks after the composite signal activates. Source: Food Banks Canada Annual Report 2024.
